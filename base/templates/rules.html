Vehicle Rental Rules & Regulations

  1. The renter must possess a valid driving license at the time of vehicle pickup.
  2. The rented vehicle should be used only for personal purposes and not for commercial activities.
  3. The renter is responsible for any traffic violations, fines, or penalties during the rental period.
  4. The vehicle must be returned on or before the agreed time to avoid additional charges.
  5. Smoking, alcohol consumption, or illegal activities inside the vehicle are strictly prohibited.
  6. Any damage to the vehicle during the rental period must be reported immediately.
  7. The renter is liable for repair costs in case of damage due to negligence.
  8. Fuel level at the time of return should be the same as at the time of pickup.
  9. Subleasing or allowing unauthorized persons to drive the vehicle is not allowed.
  10. Failure to follow the rules may result in cancellation of the rental without refund.
